Sport and Fitness

The role of ITB Sport is to facilitate any student who wishes to partake in physical activity while they complete their studies in ITB.

Our Sports Development Officer, Ronan Keaskin, along with our two sports interns Clíodhna Mac Namara and Conor Parnell work with ITB students to provide an excellent sporting environment.

Along with providing excellent sporting facilities, there are also active club programmes catered to suit student needs.
